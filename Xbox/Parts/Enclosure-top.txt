
The original Xbox console was released in 2001 and was a large black box with a green X on top1.
The Xbox 360, released in 2005, was smaller and more streamlined than its predecessor2.
The Xbox One, released in 2013, was larger than the Xbox 360 and had a more rectangular shape3.
The Xbox Series X, released in 2020, has a tall rectangular shape and is black with a green circle on top2. 
The Xbox Series S, also released in 2020, is smaller than the Series X and has a white rectangular shape2.
